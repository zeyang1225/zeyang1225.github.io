<br> Short Bio   </h2>

Welcome! I am Zeyang (pronounced "Zuh-Yahng") Ju, an assistant professor at the School of Accountancy and Institute of Accounting and Finance, Shanghai University of Finance and Economics. I use archival and field methods to understand real-world accounting issues in the context of digital technology and sustainability. I earned a Ph.D. in accounting from Univerisity of Kentucky, a research master’s degree with Chancellor’s Commendation for Academic Excellence from the Australian National University, and a bachelor’s degree from Beijing Institute of Technology.
