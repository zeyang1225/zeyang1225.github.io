<br> Short Bio   </h2>

Welcome! I am Zeyang (pronounced "Zuh-Yahng") Ju, an assistant professor at the Institute of Accounting and Finance and School of Accountancy, Shanghai University of Finance and Economics. I use archival and field methods to understand real-world accounting issues in the context of digital technology and sustainability. I hold a Ph.D. in accounting from University of Kentucky, a research master’s degree with Chancellor’s Commendation from the Australian National University, and a bachelor’s degree from Beijing Institute of Technology.
